Irregular verb

Regular verbs are verbs formed by adding –ed to the end

Bare infinitive Simple past Past participle
wash washed washed
walk walked walked
cook cooked cooked

Unlikely, irregular verbs are verbs that do not form its simple past tense or past participle by adding –ed to the bare infinitive.
